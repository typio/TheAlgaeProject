The Algae Project

The goal of this website was to demonstrate the simplicity of growing algae with an interactive physics demo. Algae requires very few things to grow, and with just water, CO2, light, and fertilizer, can double mass in just a few days. This website is complimentary to a research paper I wrote on using algal biofuel in automative industries. I coded the website in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS with Matter.js and P5.js libraries.
